About QuantumPulse

QuantumPulse is an AI‑assisted trading companion. It blends news‑trend momentum, source reliability, and price action into simple Buy / Hold / Sell signals.

This MVP demonstrates the structure: a Flask API with a lightweight UI. The production roadmap adds live news ingestion, weighting, backtests, broker connectivity, and a self‑learning loop.
